How Our Services Work

Our chit fund services follow a simple, structured, and transparent process designed to make participation easy and stress-free for all members.

Members begin by selecting a chit plan that aligns with their financial objectives. A fixed monthly contribution is made for the duration of the chit, promoting consistent and disciplined savings.

Each month, a transparent auction is conducted where one member receives the chit amount. Over the full tenure, every participant receives the lump-sum amount once, making chit funds a reliable method for both saving and planned borrowing.

This well-defined process ensures fairness, flexibility, and financial stability for all our members.
