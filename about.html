About Velu Chits

Who We Are

Velu Chit Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted financial services company with over five decades of experience helping individuals and families build disciplined savings and long-term financial stability.

Our operations are founded on transparency, integrity, and compliance, ensuring peace of mind and consistent value for every customer.

Through ethical practices and customer-focused service, we have built long-standing relationships based on trust and reliability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and families by providing reliable, secure, and flexible chit fund solutions that support their diverse financial needs.

We promote disciplined saving habits while offering timely access to funds through transparent and well-regulated processes. By combining experience with a strong customer-first approach, we strive to be a dependable financial partner at every stage of life.

Through ethical practices and continuous improvement, we remain committed to building long-term relationships based on trust, accountability, and financial confidence.
